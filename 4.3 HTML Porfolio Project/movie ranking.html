The best movies according to Kushank

The top three movies of all-time

A Silent voice

A powerful, deeply moving story about a former bully who seeks redemption
and tries to make amends with the deaf girl whose life he once made miserable.

The Conjuring

A terrifying paranormal investigation film about a family terrorized by
a malicious spirit in their new farmhouse.

John Wick

A retired hitman unleashes a relentless, vengeful rampage against
the gangsters who killed his puppy.
